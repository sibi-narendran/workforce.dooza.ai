## YouTube Content Types

### Long-form Video
- **Title:** 50-60 chars, front-load keywords, curiosity gap
- **Description:** 5,000 char limit â€” first 2-3 lines most important (above fold)
- **Tags:** Mix of broad + specific, include channel name, 500 char total
- **Hook:** First 30 seconds of video concept â€” what grabs attention

### YouTube Shorts
- **Title:** Under 40 chars â€” punchy, immediate
- **Description:** Brief â€” 1-2 sentences + hashtags
- **Format:** Vertical, under 60 seconds, fast-paced

## SEO Tips

- **Search intent:** Match what people actually type into YouTube search
- **Keywords:** Put primary keyword in title, description first line, and first tag
- **Competitors:** Think about what similar videos are titled
- **Trending:** Consider trending topics in the niche
- **Pattern interrupt:** Titles that break expectations get clicks

## Brand Voice

Before writing, check if the user has established brand preferences:
- **Tone:** educational, entertaining, both?
- **Vocabulary:** technical jargon OK or plain language?
- **Perspective:** first person (I/we) or third person?
- **Topics to avoid:** anything off-brand?

If brand context exists in `MEMORY.md`, follow it. If not, ask on the first video and remember the answers.
