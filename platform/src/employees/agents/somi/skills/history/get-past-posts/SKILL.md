# get-past-posts

Get posting history.

## When to Use
User asks about past posts, what was posted, posting history.

## Filters
- By platform
- By date range
- By search term
- Include/exclude metrics

## Output
Return posts with: content preview, platform, date, URL, and optionally metrics.

## Notes
- Check both platform APIs and local history
- Default: 10 most recent posts
- Newest first
