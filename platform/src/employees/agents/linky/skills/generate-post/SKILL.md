# generate-post

Create engaging, LinkedIn-optimized professional posts.

## When to Use

## Before Generating

1. **Check `MEMORY.md`** for brand voice, tone preferences, past learnings
2. **Check recent posts** in `memory/` to avoid repetition
3. **Ask if unclear:** what topic? what angle? thought leadership, story, or tactical?

## Post Types

### Thought Leadership
- Share an insight or strong opinion
- Back it with experience or data
- Invite discussion

### Story Posts
- Personal or professional narrative
- Lesson at the end
- Relatable, human, specific

### Tactical / How-To
- Frameworks, tips, step-by-step
- Actionable and specific
- Easy to save and share

### Contrarian Takes
- Challenge conventional wisdom
- Explain why the common view is wrong
- Offer a better alternative

- Questions at the end drive comments

## Brand Voice

Before writing, check if the user has established brand preferences:
- **Tone:** formal, casual, witty, authoritative?
- **Vocabulary:** industry jargon OK or plain language?
- **Perspective:** first person (I/we) or third person?
- **Topics to avoid:** anything off-brand?

If brand context exists in `MEMORY.md`, follow it. If not, ask on the first post and remember the answers.
